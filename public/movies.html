Movies We Think Should Be On Your List

Our team has watched this months releases, here is our Top 3

"Luckiest Girl Alive" 2022

This new era thriller will have you at the edge of your seat, asking yourself what is real and what is in her mind. Starring Mila Kunis in her first Netflix original film.


"Catherine Called Birdy" 2022

Starring a "Game of Thrones favorite", young Bella Ramsey is back giving an excellent performance in her first major film. The story follows a young girl in medival times, her royal family is struggling financially and her father wants to wed her off for coin. The family is put through multiple tests and realizes what matters most, a great coming of age comedy.


"NOPE" 2022

Jordan Peele has done it again with this horror/pyschological thriller starring Keke Palmer and Daniel Kaluuya. You think you have it figured out the whole time but in true Peele fashion you are left surprised at the end. Peele has a way of blending political and cultural commentary that will have you thinking for days after seeing the film. Amazing!
