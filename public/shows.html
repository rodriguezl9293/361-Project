These Are The Shows You Should Be Watching

Here are our teams new favorite shows

"The House of the Dragon" 2022

Following fan favorite GOT, this new adaptation of George R.R Martin's "Fire and Blood" is a new anthology series following the Targaryen Blood Line. This new series is filled with dangerous dragons and plotting to take the thrown, which is what is expected of GOT; yet this show breathes new air with a stellar cast!


"The Handmaid's Tale" 2022

In the final season of the series we see June trying to live her life post Fred and post Gilead, yet she finds herself in the grasp of Serena Joy. Every episode will have you wanting more, will Gilead be destroyed, will Aunt Lydia have a redemption arc? Must watch!


"The Rings of Power" 2022

Back in Middle Earth after so many years. Lord of the Rings hits a familiar note bringing us back into the land of Elves, Dwarfs, Little Foots, and Wizards. An exciting show it has turned out to be, we cannot get enough of this world!
