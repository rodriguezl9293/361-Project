Movies & Shows You Should Be Watching

We have watched the newest movies and shows and created the perfect line-up for you!

Movies We Think Should Be On Your List

Our team has watched this months releases, here is our Top 3

"Luckiest Girl Alive" 2022

This new era thriller will have you at the edge of your seat, asking yourself what is real and what is in her mind. Starring Mila Kunis in her first Netflix original film.


"Catherine Called Birdy" 2022

Starring a "Game of Thrones favorite", young Bella Ramsey is back giving an excellent performance in her first major film. The story follows a young girl in medival times, her royal family is struggling financially and her father wants to wed her off for coin. The family is put through multiple tests and realizes what matters most, a great coming of age comedy.


"NOPE" 2022

Jordan Peele has done it again with this horror/pyschological thriller starring Keke Palmer and Daniel Kaluuya. You think you have it figured out the whole time but in true Peele fashion you are left surprised at the end. Peele has a way of blending political and cultural commentary that will have you thinking for days after seeing the film. Amazing!

These Are The Shows You Should Be Watching

Here are our teams new favorite shows

"The House of the Dragon" 2022

Following fan favorite GOT, this new adaptation of George R.R Martin's "Fire and Blood" is a new anthology series following the Targaryen Blood Line. This new series is filled with dangerous dragons and plotting to take the thrown, which is what is expected of GOT; yet this show breathes new air with a stellar cast!


"The Handmaid's Tale" 2022

In the final season of the series we see June trying to live her life post Fred and post Gilead, yet she finds herself in the grasp of Serena Joy. Every episode will have you wanting more, will Gilead be destroyed, will Aunt Lydia have a redemption arc? Must watch!


"The Rings of Power" 2022

Back in Middle Earth after so many years. Lord of the Rings hits a familiar note bringing us back into the land of Elves, Dwarfs, Little Foots, and Wizards. An exciting show it has turned out to be, we cannot get enough of this world!
